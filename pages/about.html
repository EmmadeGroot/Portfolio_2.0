About Me

Hello! My name is Emma de Groot and I'm a student at HAN University of applied sciences. I follow the course Communication and Multimedia Design, otherwise known as CMD. Before al this, I took a gap year after graduating from SSgN with a HAVO degree in wich I mostly worked and took a lot of long walks.
I am eighteen years old, living my life in the Netherlands with my family and two adorable cats. I have a passion for pretty much anything art related. From old-fashioned drawing on paper, to working with clay, to digital animations.
